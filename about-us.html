Our Story

The Coega Development Foundation, formerly known as the Coega Skills Development Centre, was created by the Coega Development Corporation (CDC) to address socio-economic challenges through skills development and related services. Leveraging the CDC's experience, the foundation offers tailored socio-economic development solutions in the CDC's operational areas, primarily in Gqeberha. Operating from the Coega Special Economic Zone, the foundation provides mobile services, extending its impact across rural and urban South Africa.

Our programs

MATHS AND SCIENCE PROGRAMME

The Coega Maths and Science Programme is an intervention necessitated by the high failure rate in Maths and Science in the Eastern Cape and throughout the country. It is a second chance for matric learners with poor matric results in Maths and Science. The intervention is focused on learners from disadvantaged communities and is an enabler for access to higher education anchored on partnerships with like-minded organisations.

INTERNSHIP PROGRAMME

The CDC initiated a flagship internship program to offer experiential learning to unemployed graduates and students, enabling them to fulfill practical work requirements for their qualifications. Part of the company's Corporate Social Responsibility Initiatives, the Coega Internship Programme has flourished, contributing significantly to the early career development of numerous young professionals. Since its inception, the program has seen hundreds of graduates, many of whom have become influential and successful contributors to South Africa's economy.
